Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you register.


Where and when is DevConf 2026 taking place?+
DevConf 2026 runs for three days at the Moscone Convention Center in San Francisco. Exact dates and a full room-by-room schedule are posted on the Venue and Schedule pages once you register.

Can I attend remotely if I can't travel?+
Yes. Pro and Team passes include lifetime access to recorded sessions, and Standard passes include recordings for 30 days, so you can catch every talk even if you're not on site.

What is the refund and cancellation policy?+
Passes are fully refundable up to 30 days before the event, and 50% refundable up to 7 days before. After that, your pass can still be transferred to a colleague at no extra cost.

Do you offer discounts for students or groups?+
Students get 50% off the Standard pass with a valid .edu email. Teams of 5 or more can reach out for custom group pricing beyond the listed Team tier.

Is there a code of conduct for the event?+
Yes, all attendees, speakers, and staff agree to our Code of Conduct at registration, which covers respectful behavior, harassment policy, and how to report concerns on site.

Will meals and travel be provided?+
Lunch and coffee breaks are included in every pass. Travel and hotel stays aren't included, but we negotiate a discounted room block each year, linked on the Venue page closer to the event.
